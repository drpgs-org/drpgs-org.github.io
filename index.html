Prof. Dr. P. Gunasekaran Ph.D., D.Sc., FAMI, FBRS, FNABS, FTA



Prof. Dr. P. Gunasekaran, popularly known as Dr. PGS, Vice-Chancellor, VIT University, Bhopal, India, is a Senior Professor of Microbiology in India. He has 32 years of teaching and research experience in Microbiology, Biotechnology and Genomics. He taught courses in Biology, Integrated Biology, Microbial Gene Technology, Biochemical Technology and Genomics. The areas of his expertise includes the following.

  • Industrial Microbiology
  • Fermentation Technology
  • Genetics and Molecular Biology
  • Recombinant DNA Technology
  • Microbial Genomics
  • Proteomics
